**roamblr** connects traveling creators (YouTubers, TikTokers, Instagrammers) with trusted local creators in any city. No DMs into the void. No expensive fixers. Just real collaborations.

![roamblr hero](https://via.placeholder.com/1200x600/fbfaf8/c4511c?text=roamblr+screenshot)

## 🌍 The Problem

Traveling creators waste days:
- Figuring out where to film
- DMing strangers on Instagram (who ignore or ghost them)
- Wondering if someone is legit
- Missing authentic content opportunities

## ✅ The Solution

roamblr is a simple directory of vetted local creators who:
- Know their city inside out
- Are open to collaborations
- Have hosted travelers before
- Want exposure to new audiences

## 🎯 How It Works

### For Travelers
1. **Search** any city (e.g. Nairobi, Marrakech, London)
2. **Browse** local creators with their niche, subs, and past collabs
3. **Request an intro** — we connect you both via email within 24h
4. **Meet & film** — you bring the audience, they bring the city

### For Locals
1. **Apply** with your channel and city (free)
2. **Get vetted** — we review your content
3. **Get discovered** — travelers find you in the directory
4. **Grow your audience** — get featured in videos from around the world

**No money changes hands.** Exposure is the currency.
